Title: Scientists Confirm Earth Will Experience 3 Days of Darkness Next Week

Scientists have reportedly confirmed that the Earth will experience three days of complete darkness next week due to a rare planetary alignment. According to unnamed sources, the event will disrupt global communications and cause major power outages.

However, no official space agency has released any statement confirming this claim. Experts say the information has been circulating on social media without scientific evidence. The claim has not been verified by any recognized astronomical organization.
